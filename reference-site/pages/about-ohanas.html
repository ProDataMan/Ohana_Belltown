 KYLE YOSHIMURA (YOSH)

Founder


Kyle Yoshimura first opened Ohana Sushi Bar and Grill in the heart of Belltown in 1999.
​
Ohana quickly became a Seattle staple for Hawaiian-Japanese cuisine, creative and tasteful drinks, and an abundance of good company.

After watching Ohana become an integral part of the Belltown community, Kyle opened Ohana Eastside in the heart of Gilman, Issaquah. Both locations are proud to provide community events such as Hawaiian live music and Karaoke nights.
