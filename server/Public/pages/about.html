About Ohana's

Kyle Yoshimura (Yosh)

Founder

Kyle Yoshimura first opened Ohana Sushi Bar and Grill in the heart of Belltown in 1999.

Ohana quickly became a Seattle staple for Hawaiian-Japanese cuisine, creative and tasteful drinks, and an abundance of good company.

After watching Ohana become an integral part of the Belltown community, Kyle later opened a second location, Ohana Eastside, in the heart of Gilman, Issaquah. Both locations were proud to provide community events such as Hawaiian live music and karaoke nights.
