Hawaiian-Japanese comfort food, Belltown style

Since 1999, Ohana has been a Seattle staple for Hawaiian-Japanese cuisine, creative and tasteful drinks, and an abundance of good company. From fresh poke and sushi to plate-lunch classics like Loco Moco and Kalua Pork, every dish is made to share with ‘ohana’ — family.
